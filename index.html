WHO AM I, EXACTLY?

Hello, World! My name is Dinie Safin bin Razmin Masli, a 20-year-old student from Universiti Teknologi MARA . Born in Subang Jaya and was raised at Puchong, Selangor throughout my life, I studied at Cyberjaya until high school. Throughout school, I had a growing passion for video games and visual storytelling, as I think both forms of media can be fun and provocative in sharing their messages to the audience. From there, it snowballed into deepening my understanding of the games industry via self-research and extensive playing. (for research purposes, of course). After graduating high school, I stumbled upon this course, the Diploma of New Media Communications and Contentpreneurship which inspired me to pick it due to wanting to explore my skillsets in game designing as well as social media content creation. My current desire is to get a job within the games industry as a level or game designer and help to create fun gameplay scenarios for the player to enjoy. Otherwise, a social media content manager seems right up my alley too. Currently learning to expand my skillset in logo designing and voice acting as a fun hobby. My interests are watching YouTube and documentaries, cycling and swimming.
